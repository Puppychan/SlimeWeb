Rimuru's Secretaries

We love Rimuru

Rimuru has 3 main secretaries: Diablo, Ranga, and Shion. Diablo: is one of the seven Primordial Demons and an Executive of the Jura Tempest Federation as the Second Secretary of Rimuru Tempest. Ranga: one of the first group of subordinates named by Rimuru Tempest. He is the leader of the Tempest Wolf Clan and the son of the previous boss of the Direwolves. Shion: is a demihuman that was part of the third group of subordinates to be named by Rimuru and is also his number one secretary and bodyguard. Shion lives in the Capital city of the Jura Tempest Federation: Rimuru City.
